## Description
Lob (Globo) - shifting from defense to offense.

## Steps
1. Get low under the ball.
2. Use a long, upward following through.
3. Aim for the back of the opponents' court.
4. Move to the net as the ball goes over them.

## Benefits
- Resets the point rhythm.
- Forces opponents away from the net.
