## Description
The "Bandeja" - a defensive overhead shot to maintain net position.

## Steps
1. Move back quickly when the lob is short.
2. Turn sideways with the racket high.
3. Hit the ball with slice at shoulder height.
4. Return to the net immediately.

## Benefits
- Prevents losing the net position.
- Makes it difficult for opponents to attack.
