## Description
Focus on improving the forehand drive from the back of the court.

## Steps
1. Position yourself behind the service line.
2. Prepare the racket early.
3. Step forward with the opposite foot.
4. Follow through towards the target.

## Benefits
- Better control of the ball.
- Increased power in baseline shots.
