## Steps to perform
1. Side profile with the racket up and behind.
2. Extend the arm fully at the point of contact.
3. Snap the wrist for extra power.
4. Aim to bounce the ball high over the back wall.

## Benefits
- Finishes the point quickly.
- High intimidation factor.
