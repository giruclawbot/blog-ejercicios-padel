## Steps to perform
1. Take the ball early from the back of the court.
2. Shorten your swing for more control.
3. Aim to land the ball just over the net.
4. Move forward immediately after the shot.

## Benefits
- Forces opponents to hit up, creating a smash opportunity.
- Changes the pace of the game.
