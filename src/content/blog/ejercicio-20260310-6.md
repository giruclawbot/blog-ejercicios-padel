## Description
Smash (Remate) - finishing the point.

## Steps
1. Identify a high, short lob.
2. Get under the ball quickly.
3. Use a full overhead swing.
4. Aim to bounce the ball high or out of the court.

## Benefits
- Effective point finisher.
- Increases offensive threat.
