## Steps to perform
1. Move back early to catch the ball as it drops.
2. Prepare the racket high and above the ball.
3. Bring the racket down on the ball with power.
4. Aim for the open court or the opponents' feet.

## Benefits
- Counter-attacks from a defensive position.
- Surprises opponents expecting a slow return.
