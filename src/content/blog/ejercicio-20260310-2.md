## Description
Backhand volley practice at the net.

## Steps
1. Maintain a ready position at the net.
2. Short backswing for the backhand.
3. Punch the ball with a downward motion.
4. Recover quickly for the next shot.

## Benefits
- Faster reaction times.
- Improved net dominance.
