## Description
Perfecting the Padel Serve.

## Steps
1. Stand behind the service line.
2. Drop the ball and hit it below waist height.
3. Aim for the opponent's side glass.
4. Move forward to the net after the serve.

## Benefits
- Starts the point with an advantage.
- Puts pressure on the returner.
