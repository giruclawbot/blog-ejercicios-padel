## Description
The Vibora - an aggressive sliced overhead.

## Steps
1. Similar preparation to the bandeja but with more side-spin.
2. Strike the ball on the side.
3. Aim for the corners.
4. The ball should stay very low after the bounce.

## Benefits
- Very difficult to return off the glass.
- High offensive pressure.
