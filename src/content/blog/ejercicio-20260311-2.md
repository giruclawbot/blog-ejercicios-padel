## Steps to perform
1. Prepare sideways with the racket behind your head.
2. Aim for the side of the ball, not the back.
3. Use a whip-like motion with the wrist.
4. Aim for the corners of the back wall.

## Benefits
- High speed and unpredictable bounce.
- Difficult for opponents to return.
