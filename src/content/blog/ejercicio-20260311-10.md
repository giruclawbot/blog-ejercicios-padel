## Steps to perform
1. Move quickly to the side of the ball's bounce.
2. Anticipate the angle of the ball after contact.
3. Keep the racket low and prepare early.
4. Aim to clear the net with a deep lob.

## Benefits
- Keeps the point alive from difficult angles.
- High focus and reaction training.
