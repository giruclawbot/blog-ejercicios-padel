## Steps to perform
1. Prepare sideways like a normal smash.
2. Aim for the top of the ball with heavy top spin.
3. Hit the back wall so the ball bounces over the side wall.
4. Use an upward wrist flick on contact.

## Benefits
- Practically unreturnable if executed correctly.
- A high-level weapon for advanced players.
