## Steps to perform
1. Maintain a ready position with the racket high.
2. Step forward with the opposite foot.
3. Hit the ball in front of your body.
4. Keep the swing short and punch the ball.

## Benefits
- Controls the net and dictates the point.
- Minimizes reaction time for opponents.
