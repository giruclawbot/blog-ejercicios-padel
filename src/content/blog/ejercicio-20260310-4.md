## Description
Returning the ball after it hits the back glass.

## Steps
1. Read the depth of the ball.
2. Move with the ball as it goes to the glass.
3. Wait for the bounce off the glass.
4. Strike the ball after the rebound.

## Benefits
- Increased confidence in defensive play.
- Better utilization of the court walls.
