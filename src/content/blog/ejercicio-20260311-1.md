## Steps to perform
1. Move back quickly as the lob comes.
2. Turn sideways and raise your racket to shoulder height.
3. Strike the ball with a slice motion at head height.
4. Follow through and return to the net.

## Benefits
- Prevents opponents from taking the net.
- High control and consistency.
