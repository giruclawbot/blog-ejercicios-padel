## Description
Chiquita - a soft shot to the feet of net players.

## Steps
1. Wait for a low ball at the back.
2. Use a soft touch to dip the ball over the net.
3. Aim for the opponents' feet.
4. Prepare to move forward if they miss-hit.

## Benefits
- Forces opponents to hit up.
- Creates opportunities to take the net.
