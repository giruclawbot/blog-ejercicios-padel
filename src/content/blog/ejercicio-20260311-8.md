## Steps to perform
1. Stand behind the service line.
2. Let the ball bounce before hitting it.
3. Hit the ball at or below waist height.
4. Aim for the corners of the service box.

## Benefits
- Sets up the point's rhythm.
- Can force an immediate weak return.
