## Description
Footwork and split-step drills.

## Steps
1. Stay on your toes at all times.
2. Perform a small jump (split-step) as the opponent hits.
3. Move quickly towards the ball.
4. Maintain balance during the shot.

## Benefits
- Faster movement around the court.
- Better preparation for every shot.
